## üí° General Advice & Tips
1.  **Study Strategy**:
    - Focus on getting your **basics right** (usually the first 2-3 chapters in each subject's clinical manual).
    - Do not try to memorize details of every single major case if it becomes a burden.
    - If basics are strong, you will be "golden".
2.  **Resources**:
    - Use the provided Google Drive folders and the "White Army" YouTube channel for case demonstrations.
